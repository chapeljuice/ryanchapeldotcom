The web

I am a web developer and designer, and I'm proud of it. There are a ton of frustrating web experiences out there, and I'm glad to be one of the good guys, trying to take bad experiences and turning them into good great ones.

My job

I'm a Senior Web Developer and manager at the marketing headquarters of Apollo Education Group in Seattle, WA, where I manage our Marketing Platform Dev team. I specialize in responsive web design (RWD), mobile UI / interaction, and accessibility, and have been lead developer on a number of large scale projects including the main University of Phoenix website redesign in 2016, the University of Phoenix Brand site in early 2015, and ensuring our sites fully comply with WCAG 2.0 accessibility requirements.

I also created a device lab wall which I maintain for our office, and I am constantly creating mockups and prototypes of things to test or utilize in order to create better user experiences on all of our properties. Here's a picture of my desk.

Golfing

Golfing when you have two tiny kids is hard. A day at the course takes up SO. MUCH. TIME.

My friends and I have started to play 9 holes every Friday befre work (6-7am tee times, and really only when the weather is nice) and it's been working great. We get to enjoy a quick round of golf, improve our game, and still have time on the weekends for everything else.
